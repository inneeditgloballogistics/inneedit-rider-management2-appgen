# Vehicle Handover Quick Start Guide

## 👤 For Riders

### What You'll See
After an admin assigns you to a hub with a vehicle, you'll receive a notification:
```
🚗 Welcome to the Team! 🎉
You have been assigned to [Hub Name]. 
Vehicle: [Vehicle Number]
Head to the hub to complete the vehicle handover process.
```

### What You Need to Do
1. **Go to your assigned hub** at the location provided
2. **Bring ID** and any documents requested
3. **Meet the hub manager** and show them you're there for handover
4. **Be prepared to:**
   - Verify vehicle condition with the hub manager
   - Review the vehicle checklist
   - Sign the handover document digitally
5. **Once handover is complete**, you'll receive:
```
✅ Vehicle Handed Over Successfully! 🎉
Head to your assigned store and begin working.
```

## 🏭 For Hub Managers

### Dashboard Setup
1. **Log in** to your Hub Manager Dashboard
2. **Click "New Riders" tab** at the top
3. **You'll see** a list of riders assigned to your hub

### Starting a Handover

#### Step 1: Find the Rider
- Use the **search bar** to find rider by name or CEE ID
- Click **"Start Handover"** button next to the rider

#### Step 2: Fill Vehicle Information
In the popup form:
- **Odometer Reading**: Enter current KM (e.g., "50,234 KM")
- **Fuel Level**: Select from dropdown (Full, Three-quarters, Half, Quarter, Empty)
- **Notes**: Add any remarks about vehicle condition

#### Step 3: Document Everything

**Upload Vehicle Photos** (Required - up to 3):
- Take photos of vehicle front, side, and interior
- Click **"Upload Vehicle Photos"** button
- Select up to 3 images
- You can remove photos with the ❌ button

**Capture Rider Signature** (Required):
- Ask rider to sign in the **white signature box**
- Rider should sign clearly
- After signing, signature is automatically captured
- You'll see "✓ Signature captured" message

#### Step 4: Complete Handover
- Review all information is filled
- Click **"Complete Handover"** button
- System will validate everything
- You'll see success message: "Vehicle handed over successfully! Rider notification sent."

#### Step 5: Confirm
- Rider is automatically removed from the "New Riders" list
- Rider will receive congratulation notification
- Handover process is complete!

## 📋 Handover Checklist (For Hub Manager)

Before completing handover, verify:
- [ ] Rider name and CEE ID match
- [ ] Vehicle number and type match
- [ ] Odometer reading is recorded
- [ ] Fuel level is accurate
- [ ] At least one vehicle photo is uploaded
- [ ] Vehicle looks in good condition (note any damage in Notes field)
- [ ] Rider has signed digitally in signature box
- [ ] All required fields are filled

## ⚠️ Common Issues

### "I don't see my assigned vehicle in the form"
- **Solution**: Make sure admin assigned both a hub AND a vehicle to you
- Contact admin if vehicle assignment is missing

### "Handover button is not working"
- **Solution**: Make sure all required fields are filled:
  - Odometer reading
  - Fuel level selected
  - At least 1 vehicle photo uploaded
  - Signature is captured (you should see ✓ message)

### "Photos are blurry or unclear"
- **Solution**: 
  - Retake photos in good lighting
  - Close the image by clicking ❌
  - Reupload better quality photos
  - Try different angles (front, side, interior)

### "Signature won't capture"
- **Solution**:
  - Click "Clear" button first
  - Sign slowly and clearly in the white box
  - The signature is automatically saved when you stop signing
  - Look for "✓ Signature captured" confirmation

## 📸 Photo Tips for Hub Manager

**Good Photos Include**:
- ✅ Vehicle from front showing number plate
- ✅ Side view showing overall condition
- ✅ Interior showing steering wheel and dashboard
- ✅ Any damage clearly visible
- ✅ Odometer/KM reading visible

**Avoid**:
- ❌ Blurry or out-of-focus images
- ❌ Too dark or shadowy photos
- ❌ Photos of random objects
- ❌ Duplicate photos

---

## ✍️ Digital Signature Instructions

1. **Read the instructions** above the signature box
2. **Position yourself** where you can see the white canvas
3. **Use your mouse, touchpad, or touch screen** to sign
4. **Sign naturally** - not too small, not too large
5. **Take your time** - signature captures continuously
6. **Lift when done** - signature is saved automatically
7. **See confirmation** - "✓ Signature captured" appears
